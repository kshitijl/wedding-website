What is a Baraat?

My own experience of Baraats is this. The groom gets on a horse, or perhaps a chariot or an elephant if his father is rich. He is joined on his mount by a small child. Around the horse, on the ground, is a very loud brass band in uniform. Right behind are all his friends and family, dancing. The whole procession goes on the street. Cars honk, motorcycles swerve, the group moves slowly forward, continually dancing and singing, all accompanied by the band. They enter the wedding venue and are met by the girl's family. Then: greetings given, garlands exchanged, snacks served.

Ours will be the same but with no horse, no chariot, no elephant, no band, no dancing, no street, no cars, and no motorcycles.
